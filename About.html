About

Little Spoon Creations was founded in 2016 by Ann Marie Carigan. Her love for food and cooking, along with a thirst to learn new things promted Ann Marie to enroll in culinary school. A former science major, she was eager to explore outside her work in the healthcare field. It was during her time at the French Culinary Insitute that Ann Marie realized her dream of owning a pastry business.

In 2015, upon graduating with a Grand Diplôme in Profesional Pastry Arts, she set to work building her business and honing her skills. With the support of family and friends, Ann Marie was able to launch her business to the public in 2016, as a home baker. Through the years, she has come to specialize in custom desserts for special events. Cakes, cookies, truffles, and macaroons are some of Little Spoon Creation's most popular treats.

It is Ann Marie’s goal to eventually own a space in which she can produce more delicious desserts full-time. Little Spoon Creations was started to help people create unforgettable events. Each order is crafted with the intent of making customers feel special, and to put a smile on as many faces as possible. It is through hard work, passion, and attention to detail that Little Spoon Creations helps others make life sweet!
